## 👩‍💻 About Me

I'm a **multidisciplinary technologist** and qualified full-stack developer operating at the intersection of **software development, cybersecurity, design, health, and social impact**. I am the founder of **Fine Tuned Logic** — a brand built on the belief that the systems we interact with daily, whether digital, physical, or human, should be finely tuned to genuinely shape and improve lives.

My approach to technology is grounded in three pillars: **Fashion. Health. Tech.** These aren't separate interests — they are lenses through which I design, build, and advocate. Every system I build is shaped by empathy, creative precision, and a builder's instinct for solving real problems.

I am **always learning, ever evolving.** Currently deepening expertise in:

- 🔐 **Cybersecurity** — actively studying foundations in data protection, risk management, and secure system design
- 🌐 **API Systems** — expanding into API architecture and integration to build scalable, interconnected applications
- ⚙️ **Full Stack Refinement** — continuously improving and perfecting my development craft across the full stack

> *"Technology should empower people, improve access to information, and protect vulnerable communities. I build with that in mind."*
